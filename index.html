Dance Bio

My name is Guillermo Morales and I’m 26 years old. I started dancing at the age of 16 and have not stopped since. I began with Latin dance, specifically Salsa. 2 years later I knew I wanted to take dance more seriously then just a hobby. I began to branch out to other styles of dance such as ballet, modern, jazz, contemporary, Latin ballroom, bachata, hip hop, breakdancing, lyrical, and jazz funk.

I attended New World School of the Arts in Miami from 2014 to 2016. During my 2nd year, I tore my meniscus resulting in knee surgery. After 6 months of recovery, i went to visit my old dance studio to take class. Began to feel comfortable post injure and continue my training at the Cuban Classical Ballet of Miami. I truly am greatful to be able to come back on the dance flor and feeling so much better then before. I was able to audition and attend several TV shows. Until this day, I’ll forever continue my training and try to be the best dancer I can be.

Work Experience

Performed in front of large audiences, developed new choreographies, and provided private dance classes to clients.

Seen on So You Think You Can Dance Season 12(Stage vs Street), I was able to make it all the way to the 3rd round at Vegas week. Also was seen on America's Got Talent with my team, Dancetown Chaos. Lastly, We(Miami AllStars) make it to the finals of Gran Oportunidad on Telemundo.

Worked closely with directors, producers, and other dancers to ensure that the clients' requirements were fully met.
